## 2. Slide-by-Slide Content Outline

### Slide 1: Title
**ATM Simulator: Intelligent Banking Redefined**
*Subtitle:* A Secure, AI-Powered Simulation Platform for Modern Fintech Literacy.

### Slide 2: Project Vision
- Simulating real-world banking environments for rapid prototyping.
- Focus on security, literacy through AI, and interactive data visualization.
- Fully localized for the Indian context (₹ Rupee focus).

### Slide 3: The Modern Tech Stack
- **Framework:** Next.js 15 (App Router) for speed and SEO.
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS & Shadcn UI for a professional, responsive "Fintech" look.
- **AI Engine:** Google Genkit with Gemini 2.5 Flash for contextual saving advice.
- **Visuals:** Recharts for dynamic balance and spending charts.

### Slide 4: Secure Authentication
- **Multi-Layered Login:** Traditional 4-digit PIN combined with Biometric (Fingerprint) simulation.
- **Anti-Fraud:** Strict 3-attempt lockout policy to prevent brute-force attacks.
- **Smart Selection:** Bank Name Autocomplete featuring all major Indian banks.

### Slide 5: Core Financial Logic
- **Denomination Validation:** Strict enforcement of ₹100, ₹200, and ₹500 notes.
- **Integrity:** Transactions are immediately reflected across the balance and mini-statement.
- **Error Handling:** Robust validation ensures zero crashes during invalid inputs.

### Slide 6: High-Value Security (OTP)
- **Dynamic Challenge:** Automatic triggering of 6-digit OTP verification for transactions > ₹5,000.
- **Simulation:** Demonstrating end-to-end authorization flows found in real banking apps.

### Slide 7: AI-Powered Financial Advisor
- **Genkit Integration:** Analyzing user behavior (deposits/withdrawals) to generate tips.
- **Actionable Advice:** Contextual prompts that encourage healthy saving habits.
- **Efficiency:** Low-latency AI responses integrated into the transaction flow.

### Slide 8: Cardless & Utility Services
- **QR Cash:** Generating secure, time-sensitive QR codes for cardless withdrawals.
- **Bill Payments:** Integrated simulation for Electricity, Water, and Fiber Broadband settlements.
- **Convenience:** Showcasing the "One-Stop-Shop" banking philosophy.

### Slide 9: Analytics & Spending Trends
- **Visual Balance History:** Area charts showing account growth over time.
- **Spending Distribution:** Clear categorization of deposits, withdrawals, and transfers.
- **Goal Tracking:** Visual progress bars for user-defined savings targets.

### Slide 10: Conclusion & Roadmap
- **Roadmap:** Multi-currency support, Voice-activated AI banking, and Real-time Fraud Detection.
- **Final Note:** "Empowering users through secure, intelligent, and transparent banking simulations."
